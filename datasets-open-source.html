JustHack Inspiration

Datasets & Open Source Projects

Here you can find a list of datasets and open source projects to inspire you. Happy Hacking!

  • REFUGE Restrooms is a "web application that seeks to provide safe restroom access for transgender, intersex, and gender nonconforming individuals. Users can search for restrooms by proximity to a search location, add new restroom listings, as well as comment and rate existing listings. We seek to create a community focused not only on finding existing safe restroom access but also looking forward and participating in restroom advocacy for transgender, intersex, and gender nonconforming folk." The code is open source: you can contribute to the project. They also have a public API.
  • The city of Montreal has publicly available data collected over the years on its website. There are over 150 datasets available, organized by themes such as economic development, elections and referenda, environment, buildings and land, urbanism and housing, sports, leisure, culture and social development, public security, organizations and administrations, legislation and juridic affairs and infrastructures.
  • The United Nations Economic Commission for Europe provides statistical information on the population of 56 countries , arranged by themes: country overviews, population and gender, economy, millennium development goals, transport and forestry.
  • The World Bank Open Data provides national and international statistics, easily browsable by country or by topics.
