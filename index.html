What is JustHack?

JustHack is a hackathon for social change. Students and community members of all backgrounds will come together to learn more about social justice issues and use technology to help fix them.

Collaboration, solidarity, and empowerment will be central at JustHack. We encourage attendees to build projects for their own communities. You can come with your own idea or implement a community group's suggestion. Speakers and grassroots-level organizations representing marginalized communities in Montreal will be present to describe the needs and offer perspectives of local communities.

Everyone is welcome at this hackathon. Whether you're a community member, student, programmer, designer, activist, translator, marketer, or social scientist, JustHack is the place to learn new things and collaborate with like-minded people. We aim to make this hackathon as safe, welcoming, and accessible as possible. We'll have a comprehensive list of our accessibility principles coming soon, but for now please contact us if we can help you attend in any particular ways.
